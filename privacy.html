Privacy policy

Privacy designed to match the polish of the brand.

This policy explains the basic information Sunny Honey Grove may collect through this website, how that information is used, and how inquiries are routed for business follow-up.

Information we collect

Sunny Honey Grove may collect information you voluntarily provide through website inquiries, including your name, organization, email address, partnership interest, and any project details you choose to share.

How we use information

Inquiry details are used to respond to your request, route your message to the appropriate business contact, evaluate potential partnerships, and maintain a professional record of ongoing communication.

Analytics and website signals

Like most business websites, Sunny Honey Grove may use limited technical information such as browser type, device signals, and general usage patterns to understand site performance, improve the visitor experience, and maintain security.

Sharing and disclosure

Information is not sold. Data may be shared with internal team members, service providers who support the website or communications workflow, or professional advisors when required for legitimate business, legal, or operational reasons.

Retention and security

Business inquiry information may be retained for as long as needed to support communication, partnership evaluation, legal obligations, or recordkeeping. Reasonable administrative and technical steps should be used to protect the information under our care.

Your choices

You may contact Sunny Honey Grove to ask questions about your information, update inaccurate details, or request deletion where appropriate and permitted by law.

Questions about privacy

Reach out through the contact form on the home page or email info@sunnyhoneygrove.com for privacy-related inquiries.
