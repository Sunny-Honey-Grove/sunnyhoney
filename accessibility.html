Accessibility statement

Luxury presentation should still be easy to navigate.

Sunny Honey Grove is committed to making its digital experience more usable for as many visitors as possible, including people who rely on assistive technologies, keyboard navigation, zoom, and high-contrast browsing.

Accessibility approach

The site is designed with semantic structure, visible focus states, readable color contrast, responsive layouts, and motion that can be reduced where user preferences indicate a need for restraint.

Ongoing improvement

Accessibility is treated as an ongoing discipline rather than a one-time task. As the Sunny Honey Grove portfolio grows, the goal is to keep refining content, interactions, and supporting materials to improve clarity and reach.

Compatibility considerations

The site is intended to work across current browsers and common mobile devices. Visitors using screen readers, keyboard-only navigation, or zoom should still be able to navigate major content areas and access contact information.

Feedback and accommodation requests

If you encounter an accessibility barrier or need materials in a different format, Sunny Honey Grove welcomes that feedback. We want partnership and purchasing conversations to remain open and usable.

Accessibility contact

For accessibility feedback, email hello@sunnyhoneygrove.com and include any device, browser, or assistive technology details that would help reproduce the issue.
